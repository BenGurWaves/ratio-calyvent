Privacy

100% client-side · zero data retained

RATIO computes every typographic scale inside your browser. Your base size, ratio, and exported code never leave your device — there is nothing for us to see, and nothing for us to keep.

01No server, no upload

The geometric progression that generates each step, the live specimen rendering, and the CSS and Tailwind export are all calculated locally in JavaScript running on your machine. Your inputs are never transmitted anywhere.

02What we store

Nothing. Your values exist only in memory while the tab is open. Close it and the slate is wiped — no database, no cookies, no local storage, no cache of your work.

03No accounts, no tracking

There is no sign-up, no authentication, and no behavioural analytics or advertising embedded in the tool. We cannot identify you, and we do not try to.

04Third-party code

RATIO loads only its own first-party scripts. No external code executes against the contents of your inputs.

05Children

RATIO is a general-purpose design utility and is not directed at children under 13.

06Changes

If this notice is revised, the updated version appears here. Because the tool retains nothing about you, changes only affect how it behaves on your device going forward.

07Contact

RATIO is built and maintained by Velocity, a Calyvent studio. Questions may be directed there.
