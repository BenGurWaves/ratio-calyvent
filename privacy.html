Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Data Collection

RATIO does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All typographic scale calculations happen entirely in your browser. No information is sent to any server.

Local Storage

RATIO does not use cookies, local storage, or any other browser storage mechanisms. Your settings are not saved between sessions.

Third-Party Services

RATIO does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not share data with any third parties.

Clipboard Access

RATIO uses the Clipboard API to copy generated CSS and Tailwind code to your clipboard. This operation happens entirely within your browser and requires your explicit action.

Children's Privacy

RATIO is suitable for all ages and does not collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at velocity@calyvent.com
